ROADMAP

  • Protocol Launch - Onboard & confirm initial pairs. Calculate most efficient deflationary models.

  • Establish Core Team & Long-term vision. Soft launch for marketing will commence. Social Media accounts will be created.

  • Teslasafe Launch Campaign - Presale & Launch. Tokenomics simulations will be integrated and finalized. Frontend & design revamp. Rice Farm Dapp begins development.

  • Marketing Campaign Expansion & Rice Farm Dapp Presale goes live for launch. Integrate deflationary mechanisms for Rice Farm & establish Teslasafe Pools & begin formation of DEX & AMM.

  • Rice Farm DEX & AMM finalized. PAZZ aka Pazzive development begins. Integration of deflationary mechanism in order for reflections to be converted into USDT/BUSD

  • PAZZ marketing Campaign & Presale goes live for launch. Pazz website integrated with remaining with existing ecosystem. Boba development begins.

  • Boba design & website finalized. Testnet goes live. Pazz integrated with RICE DEX & farms are initiated with Teslasafe & Rice. Superblockchain development begins.

  • Boba Presale goes live & launch. Integrate with Rice, Teslasafe & PAZZ. Ecosystem expansion into a top 7 blockchain. BTCe development begins.

  • Quad-Token Ecosystem in full swing. Teslasafe, Rice, Pazz & USDB will aim to get listed on Major CEX such as Binance. BTCe development expands to ETHe and USDe. BTCe integrated into Boba frontend.

  • BTCe private sale & presale launch. Marketing campaign begins for BTCe & automatic bridge to other blockchains. PRIZ design, frontend and backend development begins.

  • PRIZ (PrizeLaunch) protocol launches. A launchpad for projects that can lock LP or tokens & linearly vest them. RES development begins. SWAG development begins. SUPERB (Superblockchain) development expands.

  • SWAG NFT social media site launches and joins the ecosystem to enable trading and selling personal NFT’s. RES (Resolve) lending platform should also be completed at this time. The protocol by this time has grown into an 8 token self-sustaining ecosystem.

  • SEC Protocol development begins. Seclusion (SEC) enables users to transfer crypto anonymously. The main token will also be deflationary & will enable major crypto onramps to anonymous wallets. Gaming DApps (high risk - high reward) are Completed. Bridge to all blockchains. Push for more CEX listing.

  • Full bridge to the top 7 thriving blockchains. After a few months of successful bridging, the ecosystem will absorb the top communities & migrates all 10 DApps will be pushed on SUPER (The Superblockchain). Will initiate blockchain at 75,000 TPS at $0.000001 per TX and continue to scale to 1,000,000 TPS.

  • Fund the development of decentralized communications & social media DApps that rival Twitter, Facebook, YouTube, Gmail, Instagram, Tiktok & Reddit.

  • Fuzion Hard Wallet integration with Visa/MasterCard/PayPal with all 10 ecosystem tokens + BTC. Wallet will have tap capabilities, be drop proof and waterproof and will also have a new token SYNA (Synapse) that will be used for all transactions from crypto to fiat.
