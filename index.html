FAQ

How to claim my rewards?

You can claim your reflections or rewards by launching the application and clicking claim. Make sure to claim at every cycle otherwise when everyone claims the BUSD pool will be reduced. Because of this, the ratio of your $PAZ/BUSD also lessens. Please do your due diligence and claim at an appropriate time.

What is the Friendly Whale?

The Friendly (Long) Whale is an automated wallets that does periodic buy backs when there are sells. By definition a Hostile (Short) Whale is someone who invest a large sum into a project only to sell off when profits meet expectations. A Friendly Whale on the other hand wants to hold for a longer period of time and wants to see the realization and success of the project. The Friendly Whale is on your team!

What Makes Pazzive different?

Implementing innovations such as the Long Whale and the Anti-Whale as well as having the most efficient network fee to guarantee maximum appreciation overtime. Pazzive is a crypto asset that will truly enable passive income for years to come. It’s the 3rd official token of the Fuzion Super Blockchain which will boast an impressive 1 million TPS at virtually no cost.

How does the anti-whale work?

The Anti-whale is a security blanket for the protocol. When maximum transactions are executed consecutively or when a specific threshold is reached, the Anti-Whale can protect the community, protocol and market cap by isolating and taxing a whale dumping hard on the market. This will ensure that large sells don’t create such enormous drops on the contract & protects the projects longevity.
